Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your churn predictions?
Our AI models achieve 95%+ accuracy across various industries. We continuously train and optimize our models using the latest machine learning techniques and your specific data patterns to ensure the highest possible accuracy for your business.
What data do I need to get started?
You'll need basic customer information including demographics, service usage, billing history, and interaction data. Our platform can work with various data formats and we provide guidance on data preparation to ensure optimal results.
How long does it take to see results?
You can start getting predictions immediately after uploading your data. For batch processing, results are typically available within minutes. Custom model training may take 24-48 hours depending on data complexity.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and GDPR compliance. Your data is processed securely and never shared with third parties. We also offer on-premise deployment options for maximum security.
Can I integrate with my existing systems?
Yes! We offer comprehensive API integration and pre-built connectors for popular CRM, ERP, and marketing platforms. Our team can also develop custom integrations to fit your specific technology stack.
What support options are available?
We offer multiple support tiers including email support, live chat, phone support, and premium 24/7 support with dedicated account management. All plans include comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.
