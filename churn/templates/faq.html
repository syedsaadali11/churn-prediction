Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about churn prediction.

What is churn prediction?

Churn prediction is the process of using AI and data analytics to identify customers who are likely to stop using a service.

How accurate is the churn prediction model?

Our model is trained on historical data and can achieve high accuracy depending on the quality of input data.

Can I upload my own data for churn prediction?

Yes! You can upload a CSV file with customer data, and our AI model will generate churn predictions.

What actions can I take after getting churn predictions?

Businesses can use churn insights to improve customer engagement, offer discounts, and enhance customer support.
