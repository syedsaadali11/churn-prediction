General Questions

Basic information about RetentionAI and our services

What is RetentionAI and how does it work?

RetentionAI is an AI-powered customer churn prediction platform that helps businesses identify customers who are likely to cancel their subscriptions or stop purchasing.

Our platform uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze customer behavior patterns, transaction history, engagement metrics, and other relevant data points to predict churn probability with 95%+ accuracy.

The system provides actionable insights and recommendations to help you retain at-risk customers before they churn.

What types of businesses can benefit from RetentionAI?

RetentionAI is designed for businesses with recurring revenue models, including:

  • SaaS companies and subscription services
  • E-commerce platforms with repeat customers
  • Telecommunications and utility companies
  • Financial services and fintech companies
  • Streaming and media services
  • Membership-based organizations

Any business that wants to reduce customer churn and increase retention can benefit from our platform.

How accurate are RetentionAI's predictions?

Our AI models achieve 95%+ accuracy in predicting customer churn, with some industry-specific models reaching up to 98% accuracy.

The accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Quality and completeness of your data
  • Industry and business model
  • Historical data availability
  • Model training period

We continuously improve our models with new data and feedback to maintain high accuracy rates.

How long does it take to see results?

You can start seeing initial predictions within 24-48 hours of data integration. However, the timeline varies based on your setup:

  • Immediate: Basic dashboard and data visualization
  • 24-48 hours: Initial churn predictions using pre-trained models
  • 1-2 weeks: Custom model training with your specific data
  • 1 month: Optimized predictions with improved accuracy

Most customers see actionable insights and start implementing retention strategies within the first week.

Technical Questions

Implementation, integration, and technical specifications

What data do I need to provide for churn prediction?

RetentionAI works with various types of customer data. The minimum required data includes:

  • Customer Information: Unique ID, signup date, subscription status
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, payment amounts, billing cycles
  • Usage Data: Login frequency, feature usage, session duration
  • Support Data: Ticket history, complaint records, satisfaction scores

Additional data like demographics, marketing interactions, and product usage can improve prediction accuracy.

We support CSV uploads, API integrations, and direct database connections.

How do I integrate RetentionAI with my existing systems?

We offer multiple integration options to fit your technical setup:

  • REST API: Real-time predictions and data sync
  • Webhooks: Automated notifications for high-risk customers
  • Database Connectors: Direct connections to MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB
  • Cloud Integrations: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure data pipelines
  • Pre-built Connectors: Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, and 50+ platforms

Our technical team provides integration support and can help with custom implementations.

Is my data secure with RetentionAI?

Yes, we take data security very seriously and implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA compliant
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on secure AWS infrastructure with 99.9% uptime
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection

We never share your data with third parties and you maintain full ownership of your data.

Can I customize the AI models for my specific business?

We offer several levels of customization:

  • Industry Models: Pre-trained models optimized for your industry
  • Custom Training: Models trained specifically on your historical data
  • Feature Engineering: Custom data features and business logic
  • Threshold Tuning: Adjust sensitivity based on your business needs
  • Ensemble Models: Combine multiple algorithms for better accuracy

Enterprise customers can work with our data science team to develop fully custom models.
