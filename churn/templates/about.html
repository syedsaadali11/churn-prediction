Revolutionizing Customer Retention

We're on a mission to help businesses predict, prevent, and reduce customer churn using cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies.

Our Story

Born from the need to solve one of business's biggest challenges

RetentionAI was founded in 2020 by a team of data scientists and business strategists who witnessed firsthand how customer churn was silently draining revenue from businesses across industries.

Traditional methods of predicting customer behavior were reactive, expensive, and often inaccurate. We knew there had to be a better way – a way that leveraged the power of artificial intelligence to predict churn before it happened.

Today, we're proud to serve over 500+ enterprise clients worldwide, helping them retain customers, increase revenue, and build stronger relationships through data-driven insights.

The Eureka Moment
When we realized that 95% of customer churn could be predicted 30 days in advance using the right AI models and data patterns.
