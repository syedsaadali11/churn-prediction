Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and getting the support you need

How quickly do you respond to support requests?
We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent issues, our 24/7 emergency support line is available for immediate assistance. Enterprise clients receive priority support with even faster response times.
What's the best way to get technical support?
For technical issues, we recommend using our live chat or calling our support line directly. This allows our technical team to provide real-time assistance, screen sharing, and immediate troubleshooting for complex problems.
Can I schedule a product demo or consultation?
Select "Request Demo" in the contact form above, or call us directly to schedule a personalized demonstration of RetentionAI's capabilities tailored to your specific use case and industry requirements.
Do you offer on-site consultations and training?
Yes, we offer on-site consultations and training sessions for enterprise clients. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements and schedule a visit from our AI experts and implementation specialists.
What information should I include in my support request?
Please include your account details, a detailed description of the issue, any error messages, steps to reproduce the problem, and your current subscription plan. Screenshots, screen recordings, or log files are also very helpful for faster resolution.
Is support available in multiple languages?
Currently, we provide support in English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. We're continuously expanding our multilingual support team to better serve our global customer base across different time zones.
